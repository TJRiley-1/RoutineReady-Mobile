Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to request account deletion.

What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove all data associated with your account, including:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • School, class, and teacher information
  • All schedules, templates, and task data
  • Custom themes and uploaded images
  • Display session history
  • Subscription records (your App Store or Play Store subscription must be cancelled separately)

How to request deletion

Send an email to info@routineready.co.uk from the email address associated with your account. Include "Delete my account" in the subject line.

We will process your request and permanently delete all associated data within 30 days, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Request Account Deletion

Cancel your subscription first

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel any active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription before requesting deletion:

  • App Store — Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Routine Ready > Cancel
  • Google Play — Play Store > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Routine Ready > Cancel

If you have any questions, contact us at info@routineready.co.uk.
