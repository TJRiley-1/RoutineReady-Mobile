Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 March 2026

Routine Ready ("we", "our", "us") is a visual classroom schedule application. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Who we are

Routine Ready is operated by Routine Ready Ltd, based in the United Kingdom. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at info@routineready.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account.
  • School and classroom data — school name, class name, and teacher name, entered by you during setup.
  • Schedule content — task names, durations, icons, and images you upload to create your classroom schedule.
  • Device information — a randomly generated device identifier used to manage display sessions. We do not collect device make, model, or operating system.
  • Subscription data — if you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Apple (App Store), Google (Play Store), or Stripe. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not store payment card details.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect data about children or students. The app displays schedules — it does not identify, track, or record information about any individual child.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking cookies.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not collect location data.

4. How we use your data

  • To provide the Routine Ready service — storing your schedules, syncing across devices, and displaying your timeline.
  • To manage your account and subscription status.
  • To send you important service updates (e.g. changes to terms, security notices).
  • To send occasional product updates and offers. You can unsubscribe from these at any time.

5. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, which uses Amazon Web Services (AWS) data centres. Our database is hosted in the EU (London region). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

6. Data retention

We retain your account and schedule data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (school information, schedules, templates, uploaded images) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Free plan users

If you use the free plan, your schedule data is stored only in your browser session and is not saved to our servers. We retain only your account email address and password.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

To exercise any of these rights, email info@routineready.co.uk.

9. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate Routine Ready:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Vercel — web hosting.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (connects to Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and Stripe).
  • Stripe — payment processing for web subscriptions.
  • Sanity.io — content management for our landing page.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and processes data in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children's privacy

Routine Ready is a tool for teachers, not for children. Children do not interact with the app or create accounts. The app displays visual schedules on classroom screens — no child data is collected, stored, or processed at any point.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email of any material changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
